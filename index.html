Eight years feels both long and short at the same time. Long enough for us to have grown, changed, stumbled, and stood back up. Short enough that I still remember the early days like they were yesterday.

What I love most about us is not just the happy moments. It's the way we came back to each other. The way we decided, more than once, that this is worth it. Worth the effort. Worth the vulnerability. Worth it all.

Thank you for being mine. Thank you for choosing us. Thank you for forever.
